What is Aviation Glossary ?

Designed by pilots, for pilots, Aviation Glossary is a compact and convienient way for aviators and aviation geeks alike to access our dictionary of phraseology and abbreviations commonly used. Organized into three categories: Meterology, Radio Telephony(RT) and General, our database is constantly being updated and added to by our users.
As a user, you can add definitions, edit or delete anything you've added, and access your user Dashboard..

"WILCO"

Short for 'Will Comply'. Used to indicate you will perform a requested action.

"MAYDAY MAYDAY"

Distress signal callsign. Used to indicate immediate and grave danger.

MDA - Minimum Descent Altitude

The lowest altitude specified in an instrument approach procedure to which descent is authorized without visual references for the runway.
