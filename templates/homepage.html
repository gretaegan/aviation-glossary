What is Aviation Glossary ?

Aviation Glossary is a compact and convienient way for aviatiors and aviation geeks alike to access our dictionary of phraseology and abbreviations commonly used. Organized into three categories: Meterology, Radio Telephony(RT) and General, our database is constantly being updated and added to by our users.
